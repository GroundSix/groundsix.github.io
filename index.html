CREATING, BUILDING AND GROWING TECHNOLOGY BUSINESSES

From regional business news platforms to face recognition applications, we pursue business models that excite us and scale globally.

BUSINESSES

We can’t show you everything we’re working on, but here’s a taster...

  • Bdaily

    Bdaily is all about SME business news; delivered through our website, apps and morning email bulletin. Customise your content to suit your location and interests then we will bring you the latest happenings in your region, including jobs and events.

    Visit www.bdaily.co.uk

  • Faceifi

    Use Faceifi to discover new people using just a picture, or create a profile so they can discover you. You can search the faces in photographs, online or straight from your camera to find out about people you’ve never met and connect with them instantly.

    Visit www.faceifi.com

  • Diss//Respect

    Facial recognition and language algorithms combine to create a fun, witty mobile app. Upload a photo of a face, choose to have the app “diss” or “respect” it, and then see what it throws back. Not for the feint hearted.

    Download from the App Store

  • Streem.ly

    Location-based video streaming for mobile, tablet and desktop. Share live video with everyone, wherever you are. Tune in to view what people are seeing around you.

  • G6 Data

    The data arm of Ground Six Limited, G6 Data specialises in providing data for technology and app companies (including those created by Ground Six). A large, interrogatable database to be used for targeted marketing communications.
